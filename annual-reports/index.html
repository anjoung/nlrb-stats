NLRB Annual Reports

The U.S. National Labor Relations Board’s Annual Reports were the agency’s yearly account of its casework — unfair-labor-practice and representation cases, elections, and related activity — each packed with statistical tables. This is a browse-only archive of those tables, each reconstructed as a numeric grid alongside its full-page source scan, so every figure can be audited against the original.

For union-election data, better sources exist. These tables are the reports’ aggregate election counts; for case- or election-level data, see LaborData’s NLRB election datasets and Thomas Holmes’s NLRB election records (1977–1999).

A · marks a cell whose original content could not be normalized to a number — check the source image. Blank cells were empty in the source.
