NLRB Annual Reports

This is a browse-only archive of statistical tables extracted from the National Labor Relations Board’s historical annual reports. Each table is reconstructed as a numeric grid alongside its full-page source image, so every figure can be audited against the original scan.

A · marks a cell whose original content could not be normalized to a number — check the source image. Blank cells were empty in the source.
